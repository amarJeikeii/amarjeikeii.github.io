About Superstar YG

Welcome to Superstar YG Entertainment.

The mobile game, which will feature the music of BLACKPINK and other YG Entertainment artists, is available for pre-order already on the App Store and Google Play. Apart from the music of BLACKPINK, SuperStar YG will also feature the hits of other YG Entertainment artists such as BIGBANG, iKON, Sechs Kies, AKMU, WINNER, and TREASURE.

Not only will fans enjoy playing the rhythm game to the beat of these K-pop acts’ hits, but also they can collect various artist cards and upgrade to more beautiful and powerful ones. The cards will be available in various themes.
